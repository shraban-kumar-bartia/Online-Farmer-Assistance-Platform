FENUGREEK

Botanical Name:Trigonella foenum-graecum

Sun Exposure: Full Sun

Plant Type: Fruit

Soil pH: Neutral

Bloom Time: Summer, Fall

Flower Color: Pink

Hardiness Zones: 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8

Where We Grow

Site Selection

  • Sunlight: Fenugreek prefers full sun, ideally 6-8 hours daily.
  • Soil: Opt for well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 6.0-7.0.

Land Preparation

  • Tilling: Thoroughly till the soil to a fine texture.
  • Organic Matter: Amend the soil with well-rotted compost or manure for fertility.
  • Bed Formation: Create raised beds if drainage is an issue.

Climate

  • Temperature: Fenugreek thrives in cool to moderate temperatures (60-75°F / 15-24°C). It's a cool-season crop.
  • Frost: Fenugreek is somewhat frost-tolerant.
